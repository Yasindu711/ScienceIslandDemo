Discover Science Island

Step into an extraordinary world where fun meets learning. Science Island, a one-of-a-kind online adventure, invites kids to explore STEM subjects through the creative mission of fixing a 100-year-old theme park.

Embark on an Adventure

In Science Island, players are tasked with repairing and revitalizing a broken theme park that has been forgotten for 100 years. Through animations, puzzles, and mini-games, kids unlock new rides and parts of the island. With over 120 quests and challenges spread across ten magical worlds, every journey through Science Island is unique.

Along the way, they learn about science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) while having an unforgettable adventure. This combination of gaming and learning helps make difficult concepts easier and more fun to understand.
